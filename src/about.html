SHORTLY ME

I was born in Turkey in 1987 and lived in Istanbul during my education and professional life until November 2016. Then I moved to Germany. After about 2 years of German language education in Leipzig City, i started vocational education again. I've been studying Web Development at the Digital Career Institute (DCI) since November 2019.

EDUCATION

After completing my elementary education, I went to a bilingual high school (English and Turkish) where students were selected by state examination. I graduated from high school with a degree in social sciences. Later, I was accepted to Fatih University (in Istanbul) Turkish Language and Literature Department as a full scholarship student. After graduating from this department, I was accepted to the Master of Modern Literature at Istanbul University, Institute of Social Sciences. However, I had to leave my country before I could submit my master's thesis.

EXPERIENCE
